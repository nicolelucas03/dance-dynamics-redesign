Welcome to Dance Dynamics

Since 1978, Dance Dynamics has been a pillar of the Jackson Heights neighborhood and has served the nearby communities of Astoria, Corona, Elmhurst, East Elmhurst, Sunnyside, and Woodside. Dance Dynamics is your go-to place for high-quality dance instruction in Jackson Heights. Our skilled instructors provide a supportive environment, inspiring students to achieve and grow.

We focus on technique, class performance, and providing opportunities for students to showcase their talent at community events and bi-annual recitals. Dance Dynamics has a proud history of helping students pursue further education in prestigious schools such as Fiorello H. LaGuardia High School and the Boston Conservatory.

Dancing offers children an opportunity to be active, social, and confident. At Dance Dynamics, we inspire joy and creativity through the art of dance.

Why Choose Dance Dynamics?

At Dance Dynamics, we nurture creativity and self-expression in every dancer. Join our family and explore the magic of ballet and other dance styles in a supportive, non-competitive environment.

Our Programs

Dance Dynamics is pleased to offer the American Ballet Theater National Training for students. The ABT curriculum includes a comprehensive selection of age-appropriate, outcome-based criteria to give dance students of all ages and ability levels the best possible ballet instruction. With the completion of the ABT Certified Teacher Training Intensive, Mary Angello, an ABT Certified Teacher, is permitted to register students for exams in Primary through Level 3.
